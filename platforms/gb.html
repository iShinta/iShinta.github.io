Gameboy

Introduction

The original Game Boy was released in Japan in April 1989 by manufacturer Nintendo. It became the first popular handheld game console thanks to its good battery life, and the massively popular game Tetris which was sold with the Game Boy as a bundle.

General

  • Manufacturer: Nintendo
  • Generation: Fourth
  • Retail Availability:

    Japan: April 1989
    North America: July 1989
    Europe: September 1990

  • Units Sold: 118.69 million
  • Best-Selling Games:

    Tetris: 30.26 million
    Pokemon Red and Blue: 23.64 million
